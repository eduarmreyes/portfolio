About

This is my journey, where I am is the strict result of what I have done; where I want to get will depend on what I do everyday.

  • 2010-2011

    My Humble Beginnings

    Studied and worked on projects at ITCA-FEPADE, the first time I get into HTML was magical, for some reason I didn't use tables as my markups like everybody else so I decided I needed to read a book of CSS; to be honest I don't remember the name of it but that book opened my eyes to a better horizon.

  • 2012

    My first job

    My very first opportunity to work on a development agency was on 2012 where I work remotely even though my employer was based on the same country, ES. It's funny because my country is smaller than many states on other places.

  • Nov 2012 - Jan 2016

    Transition to Full Stack Web Developer

    I was blessed to get the opportunity to work on Telus International, I was able to work on 50+ projects, from small to big size-projects. My impact was felt in the company and I also was impacted by their spirit and values. Great experience.

  • 2016-2017

    Phase Two - Real Time Web Developer

    I was able to land a job at a startup-like company such as WebRTC Ventures, great experience and met amazing people there, the stack I was used to were no longer something I would use on my daily basis, thus my learning abilities had to emerge. I was constantly learning more and more about WebRTC and Ruby on Rails, not to mention ReactJS and Clojure. Although ReactJS was the one I feel more natural to learn therefore is the technology I'm looking for to work on now.
